"The gates are so expensive, I don't know how to get enough "


gates look at your total count and don't cost any to open, if you interact with them having collected enough in total they'll open without penalty. If you can't get enough, songs can give max. 30 each for getting a V rank on Finale, 10 per difficulty.

"I already have the , why can't I open the gate?"


The gate opens upon completing every trial in Tamasatchi, which unlocks once you pick up the . Remember it's all bonus content! There aren't any songs or progression items behind Tama completion.
You only need to pick up the egg for the TASK ENGAGED door.

"How am I supposed to get around the trash can below Ops:Limone?"


Going up the ladder to the ring around the song will move the trash can out of the way. If you've reached Final Landing/Wave 2 there will be footsteps to indicate the path through the trash can.

"How do I get to the dark room in a wall in the Archives?"


In the room with Seraphiel, walk over a strange tile next to a bookshelf, walk back to where the dark room's entrance is, the wall will be darkened and you'll be able to walk through.

"Where can I find the Void Lens/B3611V?"


Play Memories Sacrafice in Boundary Shatter. You'll notice that the usual indecipherable text where the name of the one speaking to you would be has been replaced with the string "VEhFIFRJVExFIEJFVFJBWVMgWU9V".



This translates to "THE TITLE BETRAYS YOU" in Base64.
Equip the "Sacraficial" title you obtain from doing this and replay the song, now it'll say "RklORCBNRSBJTiBUSEUgVk9JRA==".

Or, "FIND ME IN THE VOID", telling you to go search Betweenspace.
Look for the ??? NPC in the left puzzle area of Layer 2, talk to them and they'll tell you what you need to know (exit and re-open Betweenspace for the new UI element to appear). Now that you have the VTC clock , you can start searching for the Void Lens or use this tool (by BlockOG, edit of arcorann's fiddle) if you don't feel like looking to get the location immediately.
The Lens changes its location every VTC, or every 30 minutes and 50 seconds, though it can only spawn in one of eight rooms. VTCs are universal, so re-opening the game or another instance won't reset the timer.

Picking up the Lens will open up four rifts across Betweenspace with new areas in the True Void, each with new logs and a song. Completing all four of these songs will cause a fifth rift to appear containing B3611V locked behind a requirement, which differs per difficulty.

"How do I unlock the scroll icon on the TASK ENGAGED door?"


After completing the Unity Nexus, a ladder to a window will appear in Layer 2 upper outside the room with the 600 gate, leading to a new area entirely. Continue down the path linearly and you'll reach the song Solomon's Seal, which unlocks the scroll icon for the TASK ENGAGED door.
